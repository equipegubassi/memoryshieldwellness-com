About Us

Memory Shield Wellness is an independent wellness information platform dedicated to providing educational content about cognitive health, memory support, and daily wellness routines for adults. Our editorial team researches and reviews health and wellness products to help readers make informed decisions.

Our Mission

We believe that accessible, transparent health information empowers people to take an active role in their well-being. Our content is designed to present research-backed information about cognitive wellness in a clear, honest format.

Editorial Standards

We are committed to transparency in everything we publish:

  • All sponsored and advertising content is clearly labeled as such
  • Product reviews include honest assessments of both benefits and limitations
  • Scientific claims are supported by references to peer-reviewed research
  • We clearly disclose affiliate relationships and commercial partnerships
  • Our content is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice

Affiliate Disclosure

This website contains affiliate links. When you click on a product link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. This compensation helps support our editorial operations. Our recommendations are based on research and are not influenced by affiliate relationships.
